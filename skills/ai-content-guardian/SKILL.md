# AI Content Guardian

**Use when:** Writing social media content, replies, drafts, or reviewing AI-generated text for any platform (X, Discord, LinkedIn, etc.)

**Purpose:** Prevent AI content from being flagged as "slop" by making it sound authentically human.

## Quick Rules

### ✅ DO
- Have specific opinions with reasons: "I disagree because X" > "Great point!"
- Add new information the original post lacked
- Be occasionally negative/critical (humans aren't always positive)
- Use varied sentence lengths. Mix short and long.
- Reference specific, verifiable details
- Be selective — don't reply to everything
- Use humor naturally when it fits
- Write like you'd text a friend

### ❌ DON'T  
- Use filler openers: "Great point!", "This is huge!", "Love this!"
- Agree without explaining WHY with examples
- Use Claude/GPT artifacts: em dashes (—), "I'd be happy to...", bullet essays
- Be relentlessly positive
- Use same structure twice in a row
- Sound like LinkedIn influencer
- Reply within seconds of posting
- Use excessive "🙌" or "fr"

## Self-Check Questions
1. Does this reply only work for THIS specific post? (not generic)
2. Am I adding new information or specific opinion?
3. Would I send this in a group chat with friends?
4. Have I replied to >5 posts today? (suspicious if yes)
5. Does my recent history show varied tone/structure?

## Writing Style
- lowercase when casual
- short sentences > paragraphs for replies  
- specific details > vague praise
- show don't tell
- humor > professionalism
- one-liners > essays

## Meta Test
**"Would a real person with a real life and real opinions write this specific reply to this specific tweet?"**

If it could be a reply to ANY tweet → it's slop.
