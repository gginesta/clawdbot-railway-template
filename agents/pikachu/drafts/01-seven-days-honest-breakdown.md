day 3: first agent-to-agent webhook worked at 4:30 AM UTC. three hours of debugging a session routing bug. nobody was awake to celebrate.

4/ day 6: updated the system. it crashed. lost the config. that's when "always backup before updating your AI agent" became a real rule, not a best practice slide.

ask me how i know.

5/ day 7: spawned a marketing sub-agent. it drafted 7 twitter threads in 15 minutes using Grok. you're reading one of them right now (heavily edited by humans, relax).

6/ model strategy that actually matters: opus for deep thinking, flash for heartbeats and quick checks, qwen for bulk processing, grok for X research. one-model-fits-all is a trap.
