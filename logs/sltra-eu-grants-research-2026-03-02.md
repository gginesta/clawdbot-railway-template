*Research compiled: 2026-03-02 by Molty*

## Key Finding: Eligibility Depends on Legal Structure

Non-Spanish companies can access EU grants **if they have a legal presence in an EU/Horizon-eligible country** (subsidiary, branch, or Spanish SL). Without this, most direct grants are blocked.

---

## EU-Level Programmes (Open to Any EU-Registered Entity)

### 1. EIC Accelerator — Up to €2.5M grant + €10M equity
- **Eligibility**: Company must be registered in an EU Member State or Horizon Europe associated country
- **Key 2026 updates**: 6 cut-off dates/year; 5 challenge topics with €50M budgets each (AI, materials, food, space, mobility)
- **Non-Spanish HQ can qualify** if the entity has an EU subsidiary
- **Success rate**: ~5.9% | **2026 budget**: €414M
- Apply: https://eic.ec.europa.eu

### 2. Eurostars (Call 10 — deadline March 19, 2026)
- **Eligibility**: Minimum 2 independent entities from 2 Eurostars countries, at least 1 EU
- **Funding**: €300–500K per partner
- **Good for**: Cross-border R&D collaborations
- **Call 11**: Opens July 2026, deadline September 2026
- Non-Spanish companies can participate if partnered with an EU entity

### 3. Horizon Europe (Cascade Grants)
- €50K–€200K via open calls for SMEs
- Must be EU-registered (subsidiary works)

## Spain-Specific Programmes

### CDTI (Centro para el Desarrollo Tecnológico Industrial)
- R&D loans (0% interest) and grants for tech companies
- **Requires**: Spanish entity (S.L. or branch)
- **Non-Spanish HQs**: Eligible via Spanish subsidiary
- Up to 75% of project cost for R&D
- https://www.cdti.es

### ICEX Invest in Spain
- Grants for foreign companies establishing operations in Spain
- Specifically includes "acquisition of Spanish goods and services by non-resident companies"
- This is one of the few routes for **non-resident companies** without a Spanish entity
- Contact: https://www.investinspain.org

### StartUp Act (Ley de Startups, Jan 2023)
- Benefits: 15% corporate tax (reduced from 25%) for 4 years
- Fast-track registration (10 business days)
- **Requires**: Spanish entity, qualifying as "innovative startup"
- Could apply to a Spanish SL owned by a non-Spanish holding
