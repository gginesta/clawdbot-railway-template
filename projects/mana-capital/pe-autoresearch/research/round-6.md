## INVESTMENT MEMO #1: PEST CONTROL PLATFORM (WINNER)

### Sector & Geography
**Primary:** Residential + commercial pest control services
**Primary Geography:** US Sun Belt (Texas, Florida, Carolinas, Southeast) — highest pest activity, fastest-growing demographics
**Secondary:** Latin America (Colombia, Mexico, Chile) — $2.64B market, 5.7% CAGR, significantly lower acquisition multiples than US

### Target Profile
- **Platform acquisition:** $12-20M revenue, 28%+ EBITDA margin, founder-owned, 70%+ recurring contracts, <2% monthly attrition
- **Bolt-on targets:** $1-5M revenue companies in same or adjacent markets at 3-4x EBITDA
- **Avoid:** PE-backed platforms (multiple compression already baked in), single large commercial client concentration, non-recurring service-heavy businesses
- **Ideal seller:** Age 55-70 founder; 20+ years in market; wants to step back but keep equity stake

### Entry Valuation Range
- Platform: 5-6x EBITDA ($12-15M EBITDA implies $60-75M — too large; target $3-5M EBITDA platform at 5-6x = $15-25M)
- Bolt-ons: 3-4x EBITDA ($400K-$1M EBITDA each = $1.2-4M per bolt-on)
- Total deployment: $50M across 1 platform ($20-25M) + 6-8 bolt-ons ($25-30M)

5. **Recession-proof:** Only -2.2% revenue decline in 2008-09; residential surged in 2020 COVID
6. **Exit optionality:** At Year 7-10, platform at $40M+ EBITDA could exit at 12-16x = $480M-$640M on $50M invested

**Sector:** Independently-owned funeral homes; human death care services
**Geography:** US suburban and small-city markets (not major urban — SCI/Dignity dominate there)
**Secondary:** Colombia, Chile (lower competition, fragmented, aging demographics)

- **Characteristics:** Community-established 15+ year brand; own real estate; mix of traditional + cremation; retiring founder; licensed funeral directors on staff (not just founder)
- **Portfolio approach:** 12-15 acquisitions in 3-4 geographic clusters (not scattered nationally)
- **Avoid:** Urban locations (SCI competition); single-location businesses in declining population areas; founder IS the only funeral director

- **CEO profile:** Former VP Operations or Regional MD at SCI/Carriage/Foundation Partners; knows consolidation, community preservation

### Why This Beats the Alternatives
1. **Zero customer concentration:** Literally impossible — no repeat customers
2. **Strongest structural tailwind:** Baby boomer deaths increasing 1.3%+ per year; 30,000 more deaths in 2024 vs 2023
3. **50% of owners retiring by 2028:** Best acquisition window in 30 years
4. **Real estate optionality:** Balance sheet protection; sale-leaseback to fund additional acquisitions
5. **Ultimate recession-proof:** Death rates are independent of GDP
6. **Local monopoly dynamics:** 16% of families ever compare-shop; brand stickiness is extraordinary
