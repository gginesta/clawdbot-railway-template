## Sector Analysis

### 1. Pest Control Services
- **Entry multiple:** 3.26-6.5x EBITDA (small/mid), 8-12x for larger platforms
- **EBITDA margin:** 25-35% for well-run operators
- **FCF conversion:** ~85% (low capex)
- **Recurring revenue:** 70-85% from monthly/quarterly service agreements
- **Geography:** US dominant; fragmented globally
- **Cash-on-cash math (mid-market $10M rev company):** 
  - Buy at 5x EBITDA on $2.5M EBITDA = $12.5M purchase price
  - FCF = ~$2.1M (85% conversion), yield = 16.8%
  - $50M could buy ~3-4 platforms or 1 platform at $12M + bolt-ons
- **Score components:**
  - Yield (35%): 18% estimated Yr3 → score ~82
  - Predictability (20%): 75-85% recurring → score 85
  - Entry multiple (15%): 5x EBITDA → attractive → score 80
  - Operator risk (10%): Moderate (specialist knowledge needed) → score 65
  - Recession resilience (10%): Essential service, resilient 2008/2020 → score 85
  - Scalability (10%): Excellent bolt-on story → score 90
- **Composite score:** 0.35×82 + 0.20×85 + 0.15×80 + 0.10×65 + 0.10×85 + 0.10×90 = **82.2**
