**Net carbs = Total carbs − Fibre**

## Meal Structure (12:30pm–8:00pm window)

### Meal 1 — Break Fast (12:30pm)
*Post-morning workout. Prioritise protein + fat. Moderate volume.*

**Option A — Eggs + Salmon**
- 3 whole eggs scrambled in butter
- 100g smoked salmon
- ½ avocado
- Small handful of spinach sautéed in olive oil
- Black coffee / electrolytes

*Macros: ~55g protein · 45g fat · 3g net carbs*

**Option B — Steak + Eggs**
- 150g ribeye or striploin
- 2 whole eggs
- Sautéed mushrooms + butter
- Green salad with olive oil + ACV dressing

*Macros: ~65g protein · 50g fat · 4g net carbs*

**Option C — Ground beef bowl**
- 200g 80/20 ground beef
- 2 eggs over easy
- Butter-fried cabbage or bok choy
- Sesame oil + soy sauce (check carbs — use sparingly)

*Macros: ~60g protein · 55g fat · 5g net carbs*

---

### Meal 2 — Mid-Afternoon (3:30–4:00pm)
*Lighter. Keeps you fuelled without spiking insulin mid-afternoon.*

**Option A — Cheese + Nuts**
- 60g aged cheddar or brie
- 30g macadamia or walnuts
- 4–5 olives

*Macros: ~15g protein · 35g fat · 3g net carbs*

**Option B — Tuna salad**
- 1 tin tuna in olive oil
- 1 tbsp mayo (full fat)
- Cucumber slices
- Celery

*Macros: ~30g protein · 20g fat · 2g net carbs*

**Option C — Protein shake (keto)**
- 1 scoop whey isolate (unflavoured or vanilla)
- 250ml full fat coconut milk
- 1 tbsp MCT oil
- Ice

*Macros: ~35g protein · 25g fat · 3g net carbs*

---

### Meal 3 — Dinner (7:00–7:30pm)
*Largest meal. Protein-forward. Close eating window by 8pm.*

**Option A — Salmon + Veg**
- 200g salmon fillet (skin on, pan-seared in butter)
- Asparagus or broccolini roasted in olive oil
- Lemon + capers

*Macros: ~50g protein · 45g fat · 5g net carbs*

**Option B — Chicken thighs + cauliflower**
- 250g skin-on chicken thighs (roasted)
- Cauliflower mash (butter + cream, no potato)
- Green beans in garlic butter

*Macros: ~55g protein · 40g fat · 7g net carbs*

**Option C — Beef + greens (HK-friendly)**
- 200g beef slices (hot pot style) cooked in bone broth
- Bok choy, spinach, Chinese broccoli
- Sesame oil + ginger dressing

*Macros: ~55g protein · 35g fat · 6g net carbs*

## Meal Prep (Sunday ~45 min)

1. **Hard boil 6 eggs** — grab-and-go snack all week
2. **Roast chicken thighs** (batch × 4–6) — refrigerate, reheat for dinner
3. **Cauliflower mash** — make 2 portions, refrigerate
4. **Wash and portion greens** — ready to sauté in 2 mins
5. **Pre-portion nuts + cheese** — snack bags for mid-afternoon

**Time saved daily:** ~20 mins/day

---

## Key Rules

1. **Break fast with protein first** — stabilises blood sugar coming off the fast
2. **Fat is your fuel** — don't fear it. If hungry, add fat, not carbs
3. **Electrolytes daily** — sodium, magnesium, potassium. Keto flushes them. Add salt to meals, magnesium before bed
4. **On heavy training days** — can go up to 2,400 kcal; add an extra protein source
5. **HK eating out:** Sashimi, grilled meats, stir-fried greens (no sauce/rice), hot pot (no dipping sauces with sugar) — all work

---

## What to Watch
- **Hidden carbs:** Soy sauce, oyster sauce, marinades — check labels
- **Dairy tolerance:** Some people stall on too much cheese/cream — observe after 2 weeks
- **Keto flu first week:** Headaches + fatigue = electrolyte issue, not willpower. Salt everything.
