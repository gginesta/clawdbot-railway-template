## (C) Guillermo persona + high-level LinkedIn strategy (3–5 pillars)

### Inferred persona (Managing Partner APAC @ Brinc)
- **Primary value:** credible bridge between **large corporates / government** and **startup ecosystems** across APAC.
- **Differentiation vs “generic VC”:** programmatic execution (scouting → pilots → investment structures) + cross-border pattern recognition (HK/VN/SG/PH).
- **Tone fit:** pragmatic, operator-friendly, low-hype; uses evidence, examples, and practical takeaways.

### Strategy: “APAC Innovation Operator” (value-first, decision-maker friendly)

**Goal:** become the *most useful* LinkedIn follow for APAC corporate innovation and public-sector ecosystem leaders—so inbound DMs become warm.

#### Pillar 1 — APAC Market Views (must include charts/infographics/data)
- “What’s changing *this quarter* in APAC innovation?”
- Use simple visuals: bar charts, 2x2s, heatmaps (HK/VN/SG/PH focus).
- Always include: **1 implication for corporates**, **1 implication for government**, **1 implication for founders**.

#### Pillar 2 — Corporate Innovation Playbooks (how-to, not theory)
- Procurement-compatible pilots, governance, KPIs, why POCs fail, how to run scouting.
- Speak to Heads of Innovation, Strategy, Digital, Sustainability; also regulators/economic development bodies.

#### Pillar 3 — Ecosystem & Partnership Intelligence (HK/VN/SG/PH)
- Field notes from meetings/events (without breaching confidentiality).
- Map who’s building what: universities, agencies, corporates, accelerators.

#### Pillar 4 — Founder/Portfolio Pattern Recognition (selective, permissioned)
- Case-based learnings (no sensitive numbers unless approved).
- Highlight “what made it work” rather than celebratory hype.

#### Optional Pillar 5 — Personal leadership in APAC (lightly personal)
- Lessons from cross-cultural execution; hiring/mentorship; balancing speed with governance.

### Cadence & operating system (2x/week to start)
- **Week A:** 1 data/market post + 1 playbook post
- **Week B:** 1 ecosystem/partnership post + 1 founder-pattern post
- Reuse a consistent visual template so Guillermo’s profile becomes instantly recognizable.

### CTA style (per constraint)
- Default CTA: “If you’re tackling this in HK/VN/SG/PH, happy to share a template/checklist—comment ‘template’ and I’ll DM.”
- “Coffee/meeting” ask: max 1 in 6 posts, and only when there’s a clear, specific topic.
