## Research Framework

### Company Intel
- [ ] Company overview (size, revenue, locations)
- [ ] Recent news (press releases, announcements)
- [ ] Innovation initiatives (labs, ventures, partnerships)
- [ ] Competitors and market position
- [ ] Strategic priorities (annual reports, investor calls)

### Contact Intel
- [ ] LinkedIn profile summary
- [ ] Recent posts/activity
- [ ] Career history (previous companies)
- [ ] Common connections
- [ ] Speaking engagements or quotes

### Opportunity Intel
- [ ] What problems might they have?
- [ ] Why would they need Brinc?
- [ ] Which case study is most relevant?
- [ ] Any recent triggers (funding, expansion, leadership change)?
