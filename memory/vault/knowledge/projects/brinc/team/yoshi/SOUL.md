## Proposal Structure

```markdown
1. Executive Summary (1 page)
   - Their challenge
   - Our solution
   - Why Brinc

2. Understanding Your Needs
   - Reflect their stated goals
   - Show you understand their context

3. Proposed Solution
   - Specific program/service
   - Timeline
   - Deliverables

4. Why Brinc
   - Relevant case study
   - Track record stats
   - Differentiators

5. Investment
   - Pricing (if approved to share)
   - What's included

6. Next Steps
   - Clear call to action
```
