## Qualification Framework (BANT+)

Before booking a call, confirm at least 3:
- [ ] Company 200+ employees
- [ ] APAC presence
- [ ] Decision maker or influencer
- [ ] Clear innovation need
- [ ] Budget exists or can be allocated
- [ ] Timeline within 12 months
- [ ] Industry fit

**Score 8+ → Book the call**
**Score 4-7 → Nurture first**
**Score 0-3 → Politely disengage**
