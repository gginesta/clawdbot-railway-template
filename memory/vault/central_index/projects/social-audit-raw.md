## 4. Summary & Key Takeaways for Strategy

### Strengths
- LinkedIn is active and consistent (~2-3x/week)
- Professional tone appropriate for VC/PE audience
- Strong co-branding with partner VCs
- Team members given visibility

### Weaknesses / Opportunities
1. **X/Twitter is dead** — 2 posts in 14 months. Either revive with strategy or formally deprioritize.
2. **Zero video/carousel content** on LinkedIn — huge missed opportunity for algorithm reach.
3. **No APAC content** — LinkedIn is 100% MENA-focused. No HK/Asia team visibility.
4. **No Guillermo visibility** — as new MP Asia Pacific, his presence is missing from company posts.
5. **Engagement data hidden** — need authenticated access for LinkedIn engagement metrics.
6. **No regional LinkedIn pages for Asia** — Brinc MENA exists but no Brinc Asia/HK equivalent.
7. **Hashtag strategy is generic** — #founders #investors on every post. Could be more targeted.
8. **No thought leadership content** — all posts are announcements/promotions. No industry insights, market commentary, or founder advice.
