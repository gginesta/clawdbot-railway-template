
## Email Check Format Fix (15:51 HKT)

Guillermo feedback: old format was useless walls of text. Fixed to:

**Format:**
```
📬 [TIME] Check

⚠️ [Subject] — [What they need from Guillermo]
📧 [Subject] — [What's being asked]

Nothing else.
```

**Rules:**
- One line per email, max 5 items
- Say WHAT they need, not just "replied"
- Read full email content to extract the actual ask
- ⚠️ = needs response today, 📧 = FYI
- Silent if nothing important (no "all clear" spam)
- Skip spam/newsletters/promos/Google alerts
